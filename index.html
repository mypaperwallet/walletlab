Welcome to Wallet Lab!

Generate paper wallets with AI

We are committed to providing a secure and easy solution for the generation of EVM (Ethereum Virtual Machine) compliant paper crypto wallets. With our innovative approach, we combine artificial intelligence and client-side privacy to create personalized paper wallets, right on your computer, using JavaScript code. Best of all, it's free and open source!


When generating your paper wallets, the entire process is carried out on your own device, without sending data to servers. We do not have access to your data so please follow the security recommendations.
